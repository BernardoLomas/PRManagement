## 03 - User personas - Pigeon Racing Management System (PRMS)

## Persona 1 - Professional breeder
1. Basic Profile
. Age range: 30–70
. Location: Minas Gerais, Brazil
. Experience: More than 5 years in pigeon racing
. Dovecote size: Approximately 50 - 100 pigeons
. Primary goal: Maintain a competitive dovecote and achieve strong results in competitions

2. Daily Reality
. How often do they interact with pigeons?
Daily

. What tasks do they perform daily?
. Training routines
. Feeding
. Health observation
. Dovecote maintenance

. What information do they check frequently?
. Feeding plans
. Performance of top pigeons
. Breeding records and lineage
. Health history
. Financial costs related to the dovecote

. What decisions do they make every day? 
. Which pigeons to train
. Feeding adjustments
. Breeding pair selection

3. Pain points and frustations
. What annoys them the most?
. Disorganization
. Lack of control over pigeon data
. Difficulty tracking performance and breeding history

. What wastes their time? 
Searching for information across notebooks or spreadsheets

. What information do they struggle to organize?
Individual pigeon records and lineage

. What mistakes are costly for them?
Choosing incorrect breeding pair and disorganized data.

4. Relationship with technology
. Confort level with technology (low / medium / high):
Low to medium

. Devices used (phone / computer / both):
Both mobile phone and computer

. Experience with management systems:
None or very limited

. Biggest fear when using software:
. Losing data
. Not knowing how to properly use the system

5. Motivations and success criteria
. What does "success" means for this user?
A well-organized, controlled, and professional dovecote

. What would make them pay for a system?
A system that clearly improves organization and decision-making

. What would make them stop using it?
. Poor usability
. Bugs or instability

. What would make them recommend it?
. Ease of use
. Clear benefits for competition performance

## Persona 2 - Hobby breeder
1. Basic Profile
. Age range: 35–75
. Location: Minas Gerais, Brazil
. Experience: Beginner to intermediate
. Dovecote size: Up to 30 pigeons
. Primary motivation: Personal enjoyment and tradition

2. Daily Reality
. How often do they interact with pigeons?
Daily or almost daily

. Role of pigeon racing:
. Secondary activity, not professional

. Current organization method:
. Memory
. Notebooks
. Spreadsheets

. Time willing to spend managing data: 
Minimal

3. Pain Points & Frustrations
. Main difficulties:
. Forgetting pigeon information
. Confusion about breeding and health records

Technology resistance:
. Fear of complexity
. Fear of making mistakes

What feels overwhelming:
. Systems with too many features or technical language

4. Relationship with Technology
. Comfort level: 
Low

. Preferred device: 
Mobile phone

. Past experiences:
Limited or negative experiences with software

. Main fear:
Breaking something or losing data

5. Motivations & Success Criteria
. What makes the system “worth it”:
Simplicity and clarity

. What feels like “too much”:
Complex workflows or advanced settings

Trust factors:
. Clear language
. Simple guidance

Reasons to abandon:
. Confusion or frustration

## Persona 3 - Club / Association Administrator (Optional)

. Role: 
Organizes competitions and manages breeder records

. Responsibilities:
. Managing participants
. Tracking competition results

. Current problems:
. Manual organization
. Lack of centralized data

. How software could help:
. Centralized information
. Easier competition management
